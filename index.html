How did I get into

UX/UI Design?

I love finding opportunities for a good user experience. Working in customer oriented industry has helped me to understand people's behaviour, needs, gives me an opportunity to directly interact with the people to solve their space problems by giving suggestions and digitally designing.

"Designing is not just to showcase your creativity but also to implement the design in the real world."

There is no limitations learning new things, designing was always a part of me so I chose to pursue this new path into UX/UI Designing. I realized that I strongly follow the five step problem solving process in my current position by conducting user research and interacting with the people to acheive their goals by solving their concerns, providing them with efficient solutions, iterating design, digital sketches by using softwares and delivering the final product.
